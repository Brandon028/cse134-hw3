Sustainable AI, an essential frontier in the world of technology and innovation, is the harmonious integration of artificial intelligence and ecological responsibility. It embodies a commitment to developing AI systems and practices that not only advance the frontiers of knowledge but also minimize their environmental impact. In the pursuit of sustainable AI, researchers and practitioners focus on reducing energy consumption, limiting electronic waste, and mitigating the carbon footprint associated with AI operations. This eco-conscious approach not only safeguards our planet but also fosters a more ethical and future-forward AI ecosystem, ensuring that the tremendous potential of artificial intelligence is harnessed responsibly for generations to come.

Sustainability can't be like some sort of a moral sacrifice or political dilemma or a philanthropical cause. It has to be a design challenge.
